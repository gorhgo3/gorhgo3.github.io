My Learning plan for EDA(foundations)

What is your long-term goal or career pathway?

My long-term goal is to be happy, have potential for career development, and I believe that lies in software development or possibly the backend of web development, sometimes the appeal of frontend can be lost on me with certain aspects . While I know I enjoy optimizing and identifying inefficiencies, which I believe could be valuable in gaming, software development, or backend work within a full-stack application.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning:

I sometimes find it challenging to grasp certain concepts quickly. I learn best through verbal communication rather than reading, where often times videos are more effective for me than written documents. I can focus for hours on topics that genuinely excite me, however with those that don't it can be a tedious task to research. This can be both a strength and a weakness because my ability to absorb information seems to be directly related to my interest in the subject. I'm concerned that I may not absorb information as efficiently as others in the tech industry, which places a high value on rapid learning and adaptability.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to develop my confidence, particularly in communication. I find it nerve-wracking to interact with others, especially in virtual settings where it feels more formal and lacks personal feedback. I've noticed that I'm more at ease in face-to-face interactions and want to bridge this gap. I believe that improving my confidence in remote communication will be crucial for success in the program.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this program:

I commit to being punctual and adhering to a structured learning routine. I will hold myself accountable for starting work by 8:30 in the morning to establish a productive day. Scheduled breaks will allow me to recharge and maintain focus throughout the day. Regarding working with others, I will strive to be available to my classmates and actively engage in collaborative efforts when needed. Communication will be a priority, ensuring my peers are aware of my progress and challenges.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team:

I expect the Facilitation team to be available during working hours to address issues related to coursework or any challenges that may arise. Having their support and guidance readily accessible is crucial for my success in the program.

Any scheduling information:

I do not have any commitments that require my immediate attention. However, I recognize the importance of taking breaks to maintain productivity. Planning my day to prioritize more demanding tasks in the morning and less mentally taxing work in the afternoon aligns with my background in trades and helps me stay productive. Recharging during breaks will be essential for me to produce high-quality work throughout the day.
