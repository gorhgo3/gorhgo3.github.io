From Wiring to Web:
A Shocking Transition - The Journey of an Electrician to a Web Developer

Where am I right now?

Right now I am studying a Dev Academy, New Zealand, where we learn all things Web-Development and collaboration. Prior to this, I was a self-taught student, relying on YouTube and Udemy courses. My background includes working as an electrician for 9 years, which is where my journey began.

In this blog, I will share my experiences, learnings, and challenges I face during my web development journey. My goal is to help others learn from my experiences and to stay engaged.

While some of my thoughts may not be unique, I believe my background may provide unique perspective to the learning processes of the Tech industry.

Without further ado, welcome to my blog. Stay a while and explore my journey.
